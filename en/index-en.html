what is the raspberry pi?

A computer
that does everything

The Raspberry Pi is a small, affordable single-board computer developed in the UK in 2012 – with one goal: making programming accessible to everyone.

Today millions of people use it worldwide as a desktop PC, home server, learning tool, robot brain or retro gaming console. All on a board the size of a credit card.
